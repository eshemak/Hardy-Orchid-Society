Hardy Orchid Society

The Hardy Orchid Society exists to promote the study, conservation, and cultivation of hardy orchids. We support members through practical guidance, field activities, conservation projects, and a friendly community of orchid enthusiasts.

What We Do

The Society encourages interest in hardy orchids through:

  • Field trips to observe orchids in their natural habitats
  • Guidance on cultivation and propagation
  • Conservation projects protecting threatened species
  • Sharing knowledge through meetings, publications, and online resources

Join the Society

Membership is open to anyone with an interest in hardy orchids. Members receive access to events, resources, and the Society’s journal.
