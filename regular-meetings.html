Regular meetings are the heartbeat of the Hardy Orchid Society. These gatherings bring together enthusiasts, experts, and botanists to share knowledge, discuss cultivation techniques, and build lasting friendships with fellow orchid lovers.

About Our Meetings

Our regular meetings provide a platform for members to:

  • Share experiences and cultivation tips
  • Learn from guest speakers and experts
  • View specimen plants and new acquisitions
  • Discuss conservation and research projects
  • Network with fellow enthusiasts
  • Exchange plants and seeds

Meeting Details

Regular meetings are typically held monthly and feature a mix of presentations, demonstrations, and informal discussion time. Meetings are open to all members and visitors are welcome to attend our introductory sessions.

Join Us

Whether you are a seasoned cultivator or just beginning your orchid journey, our regular meetings offer something for everyone. Come meet the community and discover the rewarding world of hardy orchids.
