Join the Hardy Orchid Society

Membership is open to anyone with an interest in hardy orchids.

How to Join

To become a member, please complete the online application form or send a cheque payable to Hardy Orchid Society to the Membership Secretary. Membership details and subscription rates are published in the current Journal.

For enquiries about membership, contact the Membership Secretary at membership@hardyorchidsociety.org.

What You Get

  • Quarterly Journal of the Hardy Orchid Society (JHOS)
  • Access to field trips and annual meetings
  • Participation in seed sowing workshops
  • Access to the Society's Seed Bank
  • Entry to the annual Plant Show and Photographic Competition
  • A welcoming community of orchid enthusiasts
