How to pay

We accept PayPal and Standing Order online. The £5 enrolment fee is waived if you pay by Standing Order. The PayPal checkout button will be enabled once a permanent PayPal link is configured by the site admin.

Please send the completed application form to the Membership Secretary — if you only make a PayPal payment without sending the form, the Membership Secretary will not have your details.
