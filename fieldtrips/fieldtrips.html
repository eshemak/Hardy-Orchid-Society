Field Trips

The Hardy Orchid Society organises guided field trips across the UK during the orchid flowering season. These excursions offer opportunities to see native orchids in their natural habitats, learn identification skills, and enjoy expert guidance from experienced members.

Oxfordshire – Anacamptis & Dactylorhiza

Date: Saturday 17 May 2026

Leader: John Haggar

A visit to limestone grassland supporting strong populations of Anacamptis morio, Dactylorhiza fuchsii, and hybrid swarms. The trip includes guidance on distinguishing species and recognising natural variation.

Date: Sunday 1 June 2026

Explore coastal slopes and dune systems supporting Ophrys species, Serapias hybrids, and early summer flowering orchids. Includes photography guidance and habitat interpretation.

Date: Saturday 14 June 2026
