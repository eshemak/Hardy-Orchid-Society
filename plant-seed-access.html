The Hardy Orchid Society provides members with exclusive access to cultivated orchid plants and seeds through member sales and our comprehensive seed bank. This initiative helps members expand their collections while supporting conservation and propagation efforts.

Plant and Seed Access Programs

We offer several ways to obtain hardy orchids:

  • Members' plant sales and exchanges
  • Seed bank access with rare and heritage varieties
  • Propagation exchanges from cultivation projects
  • Specialist plant lists from member growers
  • Guidance on sourcing and caring for new acquisitions

Our Seed Bank

The HOS seed bank is a valuable resource for members interested in growing orchids from seed. We maintain a collection of seeds from wild-collected and cultivated sources, carefully documented and stored. Members can request seeds to grow plants for personal collection or propagation projects.

Join and Access Our Resources

Membership opens doors to our plant and seed collections. Start building your hardy orchid collection today with support and guidance from experienced members.
