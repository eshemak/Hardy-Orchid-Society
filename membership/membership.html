Join the Hardy Orchid Society

Become part of a friendly, knowledgeable community dedicated to the conservation, study and enjoyment of hardy orchids.

Why Join?

Quarterly Journal

Members receive our beautifully produced Journal, packed with articles on orchid conservation, cultivation techniques, field reports, research updates and stunning photography.

Annual Meetings

Our meetings bring together enthusiasts, experts and growers from across the UK. Enjoy talks from leading botanists, practical demonstrations, plant sales and the chance to meet fellow members.

Plant Show

The annual Plant Show celebrates the best cultivated hardy orchids. Whether you grow a single Dactylorhiza or a full collection, the show is a great opportunity to learn, compete and admire outstanding plants.

Photographic Competition

Open to all members, our photographic competition showcases the beauty of orchids in the wild and in cultivation. Winning images appear in the Journal and on the Society website.

Seed Sowing Workshops

Learn how to grow hardy orchids from seed using specialist techniques. Our workshops guide you through every step, from sterilisation to sowing to successful germination.

Expert‑Led Field Trips

Members can join guided visits to some of the UK’s finest orchid habitats. Discover rare species, learn identification skills and explore beautiful landscapes with experienced leaders.

Community & Support

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced grower, you’ll find friendly advice, shared knowledge and a welcoming community of people who love orchids as much as you do.
